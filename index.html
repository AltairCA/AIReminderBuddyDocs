How it works

Three steps to never forget again

AI is optional at every step. You can always add reminders manually.

1

Capture or describe

Take a photo, pick from your gallery, type a description, or share content from another app.

2

Review suggestions

AI proposes recurring tasks — edit titles, frequencies, and due dates before anything is saved.

3

Get reminded locally

Reminders live on your device. Notifications fire when tasks are due — mark done, snooze, or skip.
