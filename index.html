Features

Everything you need to stay on top of care tasks

From a quick photo to a recurring reminder — with or without AI. Your data stays on your device.

📸 Scan & suggest

Point your camera at a plant, car part, appliance label, or anything you want to track. Connect your own free AI key and get smart task suggestions in seconds.

  • Camera or photo library import
  • Google Gemini or OpenRouter — your key, your choice
  • Review and edit every suggestion before saving

🔔 Local reminders

Schedule recurring care tasks with flexible frequencies — daily, weekly, monthly, or custom intervals. Notifications fire locally on your device. No account, no cloud push server.

  • Mark done, snooze, or skip from the notification
  • Quick-add reminders without AI
  • Tap a notification to jump straight to the task

📚 Object library

Every scan becomes a library entry with photos, labels, and linked reminders. Browse scanned items, quick-adds, and lists from one place.

  • Thumbnail grid of everything you track
  • Link multiple reminders to one object
  • Full history of completed and skipped tasks

📝 Lists & share-in

Build shopping, todo, and packing lists. Share text or photos from any app into AIReminderBuddy to create reminders or checklists — with optional AI parsing.

  • Category-based list templates
  • Share from browser, photos, or notes
  • Manual lists work without AI
How it works

Three steps to never forget again

AI is optional at every step. You can always add reminders manually.

1

Capture or describe

Take a photo, pick from your gallery, type a description, or share content from another app.

2

Review suggestions

AI proposes recurring tasks — edit titles, frequencies, and due dates before anything is saved.

3

Get reminded locally

Reminders live on your device. Notifications fire when tasks are due — mark done, snooze, or skip.
